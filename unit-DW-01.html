Process Overview:
Digested sludge is conditioned with polymer to flocculate solids. The Belt Filter Press applies increasing pressure to separate water (filtrate) from solids (cake).

Optimization Logic:
• Low Polymer = Poor flocculation & wet cake.
• High Belt Speed = Low residence time & wet cake.
• Target Cake Solids: 18-25%
